K'UN-LUN'S HISTORY

K'un-Lun (焜伦) is a mystical city located in a pocket dimension. It is also one of the Seven Capital Cities of Heaven. Every 10 years, this mystical city appears on Earth for one day. During this time, it lies in the Kunlun Mountains (崑崙山), one of the longest mountain chains in Asia, stretching over 3,000 km. The mountain range exists entirely within the Himalayas, in the Tibetan region of China.
The city of K'un-Lun was founded when a group of crash-landed in a pocket dimension approximately one million years ago. The ship's warp drive engine somehow created a permanent oscillating rift between the pocket dimension and Earth. They would appear atop a mountain in the Himalayas of Tibet. The 100 K'un-Lunian survivors created a city around the spaceship, using its material and design to build the city. No one in the the city aware of that the central hall is the former interior of the spaceship.
In order to tap into the power of the Iron Fist, warriors must defeat Shou-Lao in order to be worthy of his power. But why is that? According to Cybelle, during a performance of the dragon riders of K'un-Lun for the entertainment of its Yu-Ti, Shou-Lao went berserk and attacked the city's leader. He was slain by Quan-St'ar, who cut out the dragon's heart. Enraged at this, Yu-Ti banished Quan-St'ar from the city and revived the dragon by melting its heart and placing it in a sacred cavern. From this moment forward, in order to receive the power of the Iron Fist, each potential combatant had to defeat Shou-Lao, and then kill him by plunging their fists into its molten heart. Shou-Lao was reborn over 66 times.

OUR PROTECTOR

Our protector is the one and only: Iron Fist. We get a new one every now and then, but each of them are just as strong as the last. The Iron Fist is a power that is important to the citizens of K'un-Lun because that means our dragon--Shou-Lao--deems you worthy of his power. Our current Iron Fist is named Lin Lie and though he didn't prove himself properly, the people of Earth alongside the citizens of K'un-Lun feel safe and protected.

HIS BACKSTORY

Originally the holder of the Sword of Fu Xi, he was known as the Sword Master. His father and brother disappeared on a trip one day and cane a delivery with the blade they found. He never heard from or saw them again. He deemed the blade the only thing that could be used to find his dad & brother again and took on the role of Sword Master even though he had no idea what he was doing. He just wanted to find his family. However, this did not last as long as he intended it to. During his run with the Agents of Atlas, he took on a battle against a kumiho with his dear friend: White Fox, aka Ami Han. This fight did not go well for him. The kumiho knocked him off the cliff, shattering his blade and embedding the shards into his forearms. He was found nearly dead and was taken in by K'un-L'un's dragon, Shou-Lao the Undying. He offered Lie his chi to save his life. He accepted the offer and thus created the Immortal Iron Fist. He is constantly told by surrounding people he will never be the real Iron Fist, but he always fights hard to prove his worth. He trained for years to learn how to focus his chi and tap through the power of the Iron Fist. He was the Iron Fist, yes, but he never got it the authentic way until he met Loki. Before that, he finally found his brother: Lin Feng. His brother had been missing since their father left on a business trip and they finally meet again only to be met with rivalry between a hero and a villain. When he lost his brother through the portal to K'un-L'un during a battle sequence, Loki brought Lie through a sequence of trials in order to regain access to K'un-L'un and reopen the portal. After going through a few rounds, his final challenge was to claim the power of the Iron Fist authentically by defeating Shou-Lao the Undying. After this battle, he now finally *truly* became the Iron Fist and fully possesses the power of Shou-Lao's Chi. After this, Loki brought him back to reality — as all of the previous trials were simply illusions and simulations. They were on a plane this entire time and Lie's original goal was to see Ami Han again. However, he now needs Loki to come back with the information he was put into the trial for. Did Loki give it to him? No, but if you know Loki, you should know that's to be expected of him.

Our Iron Fist has worked hard even if he didn't achieve the power the same way the other Fists have. He has proven himself worthy regardless of how he gained the power of Shou-Lao.
